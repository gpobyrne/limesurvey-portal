UHL Research Survey Portal

Last updated: 15 June 2026

This portal provides access to the UHL CSAR-AI Study Human Healthcare Worker Survey, which runs on a custom instance of the open-source application https://github.com/LimeSurvey/LimeSurvey LimeSurvey .

The aim of this study is to assess the performance of a GDPR-compliant AI model, which we have trained to read and interpret handwritten clinical notes. We fine-tuned this model by exposing it to thousands of anonymised excerpts from clinical notes written by healthcare workers at UHL.

We would like to compare the accuracy of this model against that of human healthcare workers, in order to evaluate whether this type of AI-assisted interpretation may have a role in improving the readability, interpretation, and safe handling of handwritten clinical documentation.

If this is your first time visiting this page, please review the participant information leaflet before proceeding to the survey.

Privacy and data

Survey responses are collected through the LimeSurvey instance, not through this GitHub Pages portal.

This repository will not contain respondent data, participant email lists, exported survey responses, database credentials, SMTP passwords, cPanel credentials, or private configuration files.

All respondent data will remain inside the secured LimeSurvey hosting environment and will be exported, backed up, retained, or deleted according to GDPR legislation and standard local data retention policies, as outlined within the participant information document.

Support

If you have questions about the study, or experience issues with the LimeSurvey platform, please feel free to contact the research team using the details provided in the participant information document.
